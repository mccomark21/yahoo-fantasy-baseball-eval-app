Fantasy Reliever Rankings: Closers, Holds & SOLDs – 4/16

Top 100 Relief Pitcher Rankings for Fantasy Baseball Leagues in 2026

Rick Graham
Reliever Ranks 4 Hours Ago

Scott Barlow, Hogan Harris, Kenley Jansen, Joel Kuhnel, Juan Morillo, Tanner Scott, Paul Sewald, Gregory Soto, Justin Sterner, and Alex Vesia each had three SV+HLD’s over the past week. This group makes a lot more sense than last week’s, although there being four Athletics on here is kind of surprising. It goes to show that, despite not knowing who will be closing out games for them, in holds leagues, we at least can value guys like Harris and Sterner.

 

Arizona Diamondbacks

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Paul Sewald 70%
Setup Role Juan Morillo 10%
Setup Role Jonathan Loáisiga 5%
Middle Relief Ryan Thompson 5%

 

  • Paul Sewald has kind of been dominant so far this season, and I don’t think that will continue, but he’s certainly good enough right now where you shouldn’t have too many concerns in save-only leagues, at least.

 

Athletics

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Co-Closer Mark Leiter Jr. 20%
Co-Closer Hogan Harris 20%
Co-Closer Elvis Alvarado 20%
Co-Closer Justin Sterner 20%

 

  • Joel Kuhnel may technically be the favorite for saves right now, but I don’t see him as a long-term answer (and neither do the Athletics, and neither should you).

 

Atlanta Braves

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Raisel Iglesias 65%
Setup Role Robert Suarez 25%
Setup Role Dylan Lee 5%
Middle Relief Tyler Kinley <5%

 

  • Raisel Iglesias has had amazing results so far, and it’s hard to argue with that. I’m just curious to see how this heavy fastball usage fairs deeper into the season.
  • On the flip side, Tyler Kinley is avoiding his fastball and having great results, and as long as this pitch usage (16% Fastball usage) sticks, he should be in a good place.

 

Baltimore Orioles

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Ryan Helsley 80%
Setup Role Andrew Kittredge (IL) 10%
Setup Role Rico Garcia 5%
Middle Relief Anthony Nunez <5%

 

  • Anthony Nunez continues to work his way up the depth chart here in Baltimore, and he should not only stick around when this team gets Kittredge and others healthy, but I think he can still find a high-leverage role here as well. Long term, I’d love to see him get a chance to start, but for this season, let’s just ride the relief wave.

 

Boston Red Sox

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Aroldis Chapman 80%
Setup Role Garrett Whitlock 10%
Setup Role Justin Slaten (IL) 5%
Middle Relief Greg Weissert <5%

 

  • Garrett Whitlock has not been his normal self this season, and the velocity remains down, but it’s April 16th, so it’s still too early to panic on a guy with his skill set. At least the strikeouts have still been there so there’s that to fall back on.

 

Chicago Cubs

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Daniel Palencia 80%
Setup Role Phil Maton (IL) 5%
Setup Role Hunter Harvey (IL) 5%
Middle Relief Caleb Thielbar 5%

 

  • Hunter Harvey is on the IL now as well. Jacob Webb and Caleb Thielbar are the top two setup options here for the time being, I guess, but with Thielbar having some issues of his own this week, it’d been hard to get the ball to Daniel Palencia in the ninth.

 

Chicago White Sox

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Seranthony Domínguez 70%
Setup Role Jordan Leasure 10%
Setup Role Grant Taylor 10%
Middle Relief Lucas Sims <5%

 

  • Jordan Leasure is also opening games now, so yea this is extremely frustrating. At least he and Grant Taylor have looked great when on the mound.

 

Cincinnati Reds

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Emilio Pagán 60%
Setup Role Graham Ashcraft 15%
Setup Role Tony Santillan 15%
Middle Relief Connor Phillips 5%

 

  • Emilio Pagán is dealing with a little bit of a hamstring injury, but it sounds like he will be fine moving forward.
  • Tony Santillan’s velocity is still down, but it looks like it’s just the result of some changes he’s made and lowering his arm slot. Even with the lower velo, the stuff is grading out well, and he’s shown much better command lately.

 

Cleveland Guardians

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Cade Smith 80%
Setup Role Hunter Gaddis 5%
Setup Role Erik Sabrowski 5%
Middle Relief Shawn Armstrong 5%

 

  • Hunter Gaddis is back with the Guardians, and he looked…meh in his first game back. It’s one game, I won’t read too much into it, but Sabrowski should still be the top set up man here.

 

Colorado Rockies

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances

Setup Role Juan Mejia 20%
Setup Role Jimmy Herget 15%

  • I know what Antonio Senzatela’s season numbers look like. It’s not worth chasing.

Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Kenley Jansen 60%
Setup Role Kyle Finnegan 20%
Setup Role Will Vest 10%
Middle Relief Tyler Holton 5%

 

  • Kenley Jansen looks rejuvenated right now with some of the best velocity we’ve seen from him in a while, and he likely won’t be able to keep this up, but who knows, crazier things have happened.

 

Houston Astros

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Josh Hader (IL) 60%
Interim Closer Enyel De Los Santos 15%
Setup Role Bryan King 10%
Middle Relief Bryan Abreu <5%

 

  • Enyel De Los Santos has basically come straight off the IL and managed to work his way into the interim closer role. He’s fine. I don’t love the long-term prospects here, but for now, he’s worth picking up and seeing where it goes. It sounds like Josh Hader may still be a month away from returning.
  • Bryan Abreu hasn’t allowed runs in back-to-back games now, but he’s still not right. Let me know when his velocity returns and he doesn’t walk anyone in an outing (or at least one of these).

 

Kansas City Royals

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer? Carlos Estévez 40%
Interim Closer? Lucas Erceg 40%
Setup Role Matt Strahm 10%
Middle Relief Nick Mears 5%

 

  • Carlos Estévez is starting his rehab assignment soon, so that will be something to keep an eye on. What’s his velocity look like there, and is there anything Lucas Erceg can do to hold on to the closer role here?

 

Los Angeles Angels

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Co-Closer Jordan Romano 25%
Co-Closer Drew Pomeranz 25%
Setup Role Kirby Yates (IL) 20%
Middle Relief Ben Joyce (IL) 15%

 

  • Jordan Romano has had a rough week, and Kurt Suzuki won’t commit to him in the closer role, it sounds like. Drew Pomeranz should be the favorite for now, but Kirby Yates is on his way back soon, and perhaps Ben Joyce isn’t too far behind. It’s a mess, but I still like what Pomeranz is doing, regardless of role.

 

Los Angeles Dodgers

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Edwin Díaz 75%
Setup Role Tanner Scott 10%
Setup Role Alex Vesia 10%
Middle Relief Blake Treinen <5%

 

  • Edwin Díaz has scared his new team with his diminished velocity, but he claims he is fine, so let’s let it play out and see how he looks this next week.
  • Blake Treinen has shown some increased velocity himself over the last few games and is starting to look more like his old self. I’m not sure if it will stick, but he’s back on the radar in holds leagues.

 

Miami Marlins

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Pete Fairbanks 75%
Setup Role Calvin Faucher 10%
Setup Role Andrew Nardi 5%
Middle Relief Anthony Bender 5%

 

  • Pete Fairbanks is coming off two poor outings, but let’s add some context, as they are weird circumstances. He opened the game on 4/5 because his wife was about to go into labor, and he needed to leave early, so that was a situation he is obviously not used to (playing the opener). Then he didn’t pitch for 9 days and came in to the game in the 8th inning, and was not surprisingly a bit rusty. I’m not too worried here.

 

Milwaukee Brewers

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Co-Closer Trevor Megill 45%
Co-Closer Abner Uribe 40%
Setup Role Angel Zerpa 10%
Middle Relief Aaron Ashby <5%

 

  • If we are being honest, neither Abner Uribe nor Trevor Megill (nor Angel Zerpa) has looked great to begin the year, but I understand needing to make a change from Megill for the time being. I’d still guess on Megill leading the team in saves, but it’s obviously a murkier situation.

 

Minnesota Twins

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Co-Closer Cole Sands 20%
Co-Closer Eric Orze 20%
Co-Closer Justin Topa 20%
Co-Closer Kody Funderburk 20%

 

  • I really don’t know if I like any reliever here. I think it’s just best to stay away until they move a starter into the bullpen who might be interesting.

 

New York Mets

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Devin Williams 80%
Setup Role Luke Weaver 10%
Setup Role A.J. Minter (IL) 5%
Middle Relief Brooks Raley <5%

 

  • Devin Williams got roughed up last night, but he was shaking off some rust since the Mets haven’t had a save chance in weeks now. He should be fine here (as should Luke Weaver btw).

 

New York Yankees

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer David Bednar 70%
Setup Role Camilo Doval 15%
Setup Role Fernando Cruz 10%
Middle Relief Tim Hill <5%

 

  • Good news and bad news with the Yankees relievers. David Bednar looked a little bit better last night, but his stuff is still down, and that’s not ideal. Camilo Doval has been throwing harder his last couple of times out, which, while that won’t make him a near elite reliever like he used to be, it should help him be better more often than not.

 

Philadelphia Phillies

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Jhoan Duran 80%
Setup Role José Alvarado 5%
Setup Role Brad Keller 5%
Middle Relief Jonathan Bowlan 5%

 

  • Outside of Mason Miller, who is in his own league, Jhoan Duran is the only top closer who hasn’t had issues so far this year. Hopefully, that doesn’t mean that he’s due for a meltdown now.
  • Orion Kerkering has looked pretty good so far, and I’m loving the new splitter he’s throwing.

 

Pittsburgh Pirates

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Co-Closer Gregory Soto 40%
Co-Closer Dennis Santana 40%
Setup Role Issac Mattson 5%
Middle Relief Mason Montgomery 5%

 

  • Dennis Santana and Gregory Soto continue to share the closer duties here, but Soto still brings some more upside with his ability to miss bats at a high level. It’s frustrating in save-only leagues, but you could do worse than rostering either one of them.

 

San Diego Padres

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Mason Miller 80%
Setup Role Jason Adam 5%
Setup Role Adrian Morejon 5%
Middle Relief Jeremiah Estrada (IL) 5%

 

  • Jeremiah Estrada was, in fact, not right as he wound up on the IL, but at least the Padres were able to get Jason Adam back. So far, Adam looks like his usual self.

 

San Francisco Giants

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Co-Closer Ryan Walker 30%
Co-Closer Keaton Winn 25%
Co-Closer Erik Miller 20%
Co-Closer Caleb Kilian 10%

 

  • So it appears as if Ryan Walker is not the team’s closer, no matter who is starting the game. That leaves us with an annoying committee approach that’s likely not worth chasing.

 

Seattle Mariners

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Andrés Muńoz 80%
Setup Role Matt Brash 10%
Setup Role Jose A. Ferrer 5%
Middle Relief Gabe Speier <5%

 

  • I still don’t love where Gabe Speier is stuff-wise right now, but he hasn’t been terrible from a results standpoint. Something to monitor moving forward.

 

St. Louis Cardinals

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Riley O’Brien 60%
Setup Role JoJo Romero 20%
Setup Role Ryne Stanek 10%
Middle Relief George Soriano <5%

 

  • Riley O’Brien is running away with the closer role here, and as good as he has been, I do have some concerns with him over the long run. He hasn’t walked anyone despite having a 41% Zone rate, and the sweeper isn’t as good as I’d like it to be, but I think the sinker is good enough to give him a higher floor than a lot of over guys out there.

 

Tampa Bay Rays

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Bryan Baker 50%
Setup Role Griffin Jax 20%
Setup Role Garrett Cleavinger (IL) 10%
Middle Relief Edwin Uceta (IL) 10%

 

  • I’m not giving up on Griffin Jax yet, maybe I should, but I’d like to believe the Rays can figure out a way for him to get more from his fastball. It’s just too early to give up on someone with his stuff and track record.

 

Texas Rangers

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Jakob Junis 30%
Setup Role Cole Winn 20%
Setup Role Robert Garcia 10%
Middle Relief Jacob Latz 5%

 

  • Jakob Junis has been fine so far, and he could be fine for a while, but I don’t think this is the guy closing out games for them in the second half of the season. I’m not sure that guy is even on the roster at this point, as this bullpen remains in flux.

 

Toronto Blue Jays

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Closer Jeff Hoffman 70%
Setup Role Louis Varland 20%
Setup Role Yimi García (IL) 5%
Middle Relief Tyler Rogers 5%

 

  • Jeff Hoffman has looked better than the numbers indicate, but it’s still gotta be frustrating for the Blue Jays, especially when they have Louis Varland looking like an elite closer candidate. Hoffman likely gets some wiggle room still, but a bad weekend or week, and we could see Varland swoop in potentially.

 

Washington Nationals

CLOSER SITUATION
Role Pitcher Save Chances
Co-Closer Clayton Beeter 30%
Co-Closer Gus Varland 30%
Setup Role Cionel Pérez 10%
Middle Relief PJ Poulin 10%

 

  • Gus Varland should probably be the favorite for saves in Washington, as Clayton Beeter’s command is just too difficult to predict on a game-to-game basis. For now, it looks like a committee, but I wouldn’t be surprised if the team goes with Varland for their next save chance.
